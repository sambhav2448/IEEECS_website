What We Do
IEEE Computer society, VIT student branch works as a group of teams under different domains. These teams are led by students who are experts in their respective domains - Technical, Management, Design, and Documentation. The technical domain is further divided into the Web Development team, Android development Team, and IOS development team. This time we are also having two new teams under competitive coding and machine learning.
Our Objectives
We help students develop their skills in their interest of domain by conducting weekly technical sessions. Our core committee members develop an edge over other students as we conduct in core night sessions exclusively for them.
Our Story
We also help other student chapters and start-ups hit the ground running by building tailor-made mobile applications and websites for them.

About VIT
VIT was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with international standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis.The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research spur us on in its relentless pursuit of excellence. In fact, it has become a way of life for itself. The highly motivated youngsters on the campus are a constant source of pride. Its Memoranda of Understanding with various international universities are their major strength.
